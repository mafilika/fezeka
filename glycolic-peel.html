Benefits of Glycolic Peel

Who Is It For?

This treatment is suitable for most skin types. During your consultation, we assess your skin to ensure the peel strength matches your needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Glycolic Peel?

A chemical exfoliation treatment that improves skin clarity and texture.

Is it painful?

You may feel mild tingling during the session.

How many sessions are needed?

Most clients benefit from 3–6 treatments.

Is there downtime?

Mild redness or light peeling may occur for a few days.
