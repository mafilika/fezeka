Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful answers before your visit

What spa treatments are best for stress relief?

Our aromatherapy massages, deep tissue massage, and full-body relaxation treatments help reduce stress, ease tension, and promote deep relaxation.

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot. Walk-ins are welcome when available.

What should I wear to my spa appointment?

Wear comfortable clothing. We provide robes, towels, and everything you need for your treatment.

Are your treatments suitable for first-time visitors?

Absolutely. Our therapists guide you through each step so you feel comfortable and relaxed throughout your visit.

What is included in a couples massage package?

Our couples package includes side-by-side massages in a private room with calming music and aromatherapy oils for a luxury shared experience.

How often should I get a facial or massage?

Massage is recommended every 2 to 4 weeks and facial treatments every 4 to 6 weeks for the best results.

Do you offer gift vouchers?

Yes. We offer spa gift vouchers perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, and special occasions. Contact us on WhatsApp to purchase.

Where are you located?

We are located at 391 Main Avenue, Ferndale, Randburg. Contact us on WhatsApp for directions and bookings.
